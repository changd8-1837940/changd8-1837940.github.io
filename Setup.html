The Setup

First, set up basic information about yourself, including your age, gender, height, weight, and activity level. This information gives the Online Coaching Bot a good base for your initial TDEE, or Total Daily Energy Expenditure. The Online Coaching Bot will automatically adjust your TDEE as you continue adding more information.
