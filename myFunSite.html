The Setup

First, set up basic information about yourself, including your age, gender, height, weight, and activity level. This information gives the Online Coaching Bot good base for your initial TDEE, or Total Daily Energy Expenditure. The Online Coaching Bot will automatically adjust your TDEE as you continue adding more information.

Track Your Macronutrients

Not only will Online Coaching Bot adjust your macronutrient ratios according to each stage of your weight loss/gain phase, but it will also adjust macros based on how you are feeling, by incorporating maintenance techniques such as refeeds and reverse diets whenever you feel overwhelmed. A stress-free experience is just as important as progress, and the Online Coaching Bot will ensure that you stick with your diet plan and enjoy the journey.

Icon

Progress Update Pictures

The Online Coaching Bot uses state of the art object tracking algorithms to analyze your physique update pictures and videos. Although a metrics-based dieting plan is the most reliable method to track progress, physique pictures and videos are still honest measurements for capturing your body's changes that numbers alone won't convey. Any unordinary physique changes in the pictures may be taken into account when adjusting your macros.
